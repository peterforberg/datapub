I am not a chef

I don't have a long history with food, aside from its life-sustaining attributes. For most of my life, I never had to prepare food for myself beyond my daily lunches (which for about 10 years consisted of a PB&J, apple sauce, and some cookies) and the occassional breakfast. My mother always cooked for us, and because she did have to cook for herself as a child, she was actually pretty good at it. In fact, between her time working at a Chinese restaurant and a pizzeria, she had become quite the accomplished chef, even on our family's limited budget.

Now that I live on my own, I've had to pick up some of her skills. I really enjoy cooking, and I think it's a good way to unwind after a long day of work (or a nice break in the midst of work). I like to treat cooking as an activity and give it time. While I'm a fairly busy person, I'll make time for cooking because I think it's a disservice to both the ingredients and my body if I just fly through a recipe absentmindedly.

Anyways, this site has some recipes and pictures of things that I've cooked recently. I don't often photograph my food, and it's never to be artistic, so these are poor quality photos but high quality recipes. The reason I put this site together is because my original idea was to try and remake one of my favorite site on the web, All About Frogs, but that started taking too long, and I spent way too much time browsing its various fun pages. I hope those parts of the internet are preserved for as long as possible.
